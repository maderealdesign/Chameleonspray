Professional Commercial Spray Painting

At Chameleon • Spray Painting Co., we provide professional spray painting solutions for a wide range of commercial clients, from independent businesses to large-scale contractors and developers. We understand that in commercial environments, appearance, durability, and efficiency are critical.

Whether it’s a shopfront, shutters, bespoke joinery, or bulk uPVC installations, our goal is to deliver a finish that enhances your business image while standing up to heavy daily use and harsh external conditions.

Commercial Spraying FAQs

Common questions regarding our B2B and commercial site operations.

Do you operate on active commercial sites?

Yes. We are highly experienced in working safely and efficiently on active construction or commercial sites. We coordinate with site managers to ensure our preparation, masking, and spraying processes cause minimal disruption to other trades and business operations.

Workshop vs. On-Site: How does it work?

Our dedicated workshop handles removable items like joinery components, doors, and loose uPVC frames, allowing for fast turnaround times in a dust-free environment. For larger fixed structures, shopfronts, and commercial units, our mobile setup provides the exact same high-end standard directly on-site.

Can you match our corporate branding colours?

Absolutely. We offer comprehensive colour matching services, ensuring your shopfront, shutters, or interior fittings exactly match your brand's RAL, Pantone, or bespoke corporate colour guidelines.
