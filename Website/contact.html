Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about booking and quoting.

How quickly do you respond to quote requests?

We pride ourselves on our rapid response times. We aim to respond to all enquiries within the same working day. If you submit a request late in the evening or on a weekend, we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Do I need to supply photos for an accurate quote?

Yes, it is highly recommended. While we can give a rough verbal or written estimate based on a description, seeing clear photos of your kitchen layout, uPVC windows, or commercial space allows us to provide a definitive, accurate quote without any hidden surprises.

Will you visit my property to measure up?

Absolutely. If your project is large, complex, or you simply prefer a face-to-face consultation, we are more than happy to arrange a free, no-obligation site visit to assess the surfaces, discuss colour options (like Farrow & Ball or RAL matches), and provide a quote.
