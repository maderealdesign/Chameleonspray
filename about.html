Our Workshop & Technology

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our process or capabilities? Here are a few common answers.

What areas do you cover?

We operate across the North of England, with a primary focus on Yorkshire, Lancashire, and the surrounding areas. Our dedicated workshop is based in Cross Hills, North Yorkshire (BD20 7DS).

Is spray painting durable compared to standard painting?

Absolutely. The specialist coatings we use are specifically formulated for superior adhesion, flexibility, and resistance to weather, UV rays, and daily wear. Unlike standard brush painting, it leaves no brush marks and does not peel or flake when properly cured.

Can you match a specific colour I have in mind?

Yes, we offer full colour matching services. Whether you want a standard RAL colour, a British Standard shade, or a specific designer paint match (such as Farrow & Ball), we can formulate the perfect match in matt, satin, gloss, or metallic finishes.

Do you spray everything on-site?

It depends on the project. For kitchen resprays and furniture, we usually remove the doors, drawers, and removable components to spray them in our controlled, dust-free workshop in Cross Hills. Fixed elements like carcasses, commercial shopfronts, and exterior uPVC are meticulously masked and sprayed on-site.
