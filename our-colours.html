Premium Colour Systems

We formulate our durable, two-pack polyurethane and acrylic specialist coatings to match the exact hues of the world's most renowned paint brands.

RAL Classic Range

A widely recognised colour matching system standard across Europe. Offering a vast selection of standardised shades, RAL is particularly popular for achieving consistent, high-end finishes on uPVC windows, doors, and commercial shopfronts.

Farrow & Ball

Renowned for rich pigments and deep, heritage-inspired hues. We frequently replicate their core collection, including trendy, timeless neutrals and bold statement shades perfectly suited for high-end kitchen cabinet resprays.

Little Greene

Offering a sophisticated approach to colour, Little Greene's palette brings elegance and depth. We can match their beautifully crafted historic and contemporary shades to give your interior furniture and kitchens a luxurious feel.

British Standard (BS)

The BS 4800 colour system covers a wide variety of robust, dependable colours. Often favored for commercial applications and architectural uPVC spraying due to its standardized durability and visibility.

*We also formulate matches for Dulux, Valspar, Frenchic, Craig & Rose, and Benjamin Moore.

The Perfect Texture

Choose Your Sheen Level

The sheen level significantly impacts the final look of your sprayed surfaces. We offer three distinct, highly durable finishes to suit your style, whether you want a modern, non-reflective look or a vibrant, easy-to-clean shine.

Matt (10% Sheen)

Our most popular finish for contemporary kitchen cabinets. Matt has a low gloss level providing non-reflective depth, which excels at concealing minor surface flaws while significantly enhancing colour richness and sophistication.

Satin (25-30% Sheen)

The perfect middle ground. Satin offers a slightly higher gloss grade than Matt, resulting in a smooth surface with a subtle, mild shine. It provides an excellent balance of timeless elegance and everyday durability.

Gloss (85-90% Sheen)

A sleek, highly reflective surface coating that enhances the vibrancy and depth of bold colours. Gloss is fantastic for achieving an ultra-modern, luxurious aesthetic or for highly visible commercial uPVC elements.

Bespoke Colour Matching Service

Sometimes the perfect colour isn't found in a standard catalogue. Whether you want to match a new kitchen extension to your existing units, coordinate your garage door with your front door, or perfectly replicate your favorite decorative item, we can help.

How it works:

  • Advanced Scanning Technology We don't just "eyeball" the match. We use advanced scanning technology to develop the precise colourant recipe needed to replicate your exact hue to a 99% accuracy.
  • What we need from you Simply provide us with a small, dry physical sample (around 2x2 inches), such as a colour card from a DIY store.
  • Using a liquid tester? If you only have a liquid paint tester pot, simply paint it onto a piece of cardboard, applying enough coats until the colour achieves your desired depth, and let it fully dry before providing it to us.

Trending Inspiration

e.g., RAL 7016 (Anthracite Grey)

The definitive choice for modernising home exteriors. Respraying white uPVC windows and doors to Anthracite Grey or Jet Black instantly creates a high-value, architectural aesthetic.
