A Note on Weather Conditions

Unlike our interior services which run year-round, our exterior spraying services are highly dependent on the weather. To ensure our specialist coatings bond and cure correctly for maximum longevity, we primarily carry out exterior projects throughout the drier, warmer months (typically late Spring through to early Autumn).

We recommend booking your exterior project well in advance to secure a spot during the optimal season!

Exterior Spraying FAQs

Common questions about upgrading your property's exterior.

Will the paint peel or flake off the plastic?

No. We do not use standard masonry or gloss paint. We apply specialist two-pack polyurethane coatings that chemically bond with the uPVC or composite surface. When properly prepared by our team, the finish will not crack, peel, or flake, and is highly resistant to UV fading.

What are the most popular colours for windows?

Anthracite Grey (RAL 7016) and Jet Black are currently our most requested colours for modernising exteriors. However, we have access to the full RAL and British Standard colour charts, so you can choose subtle sage greens, traditional creams, or bold statement colours for front doors.

Do you spray the inside of the windows too?

We can spray both the exterior and interior frames of your windows and doors. Many customers choose to have the exterior sprayed dark grey for curb appeal, while keeping the interior frames white to maintain a bright, neutral feeling inside their rooms. The choice is completely yours.
