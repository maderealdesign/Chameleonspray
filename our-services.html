Service FAQs

Common questions about our capabilities across all service sectors.

Is spray painting better than standard decorating?

For cabinetry, furniture, and exteriors, yes. Spraying delivers a completely uniform, flat finish without the brush strokes or roller stipple left by traditional decorating. Furthermore, the specialist polyurethane and acrylic coatings we use are vastly more durable than standard emulsion or gloss paint.

What colours can I choose from?

Virtually any colour. We formulate our paints based on standard systems like RAL and British Standard, and we can accurately match designer ranges including Farrow & Ball, Little Greene, and Dulux. Learn more about our colours here.

Do you spray in the house or take things away?

A combination of both, depending on the service. For kitchens and wardrobes, we remove all doors, drawer fronts, and plinths to spray them in our dust-free workshop in Cross Hills. Fixed elements like carcasses, media walls, and exterior uPVC windows are meticulously masked off and sprayed safely on-site.
