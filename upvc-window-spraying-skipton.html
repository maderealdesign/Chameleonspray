Exterior Respray

uPVC Window Spraying

Skipton, North Yorkshire

Project Overview

We recently completed a full exterior transformation on a property in Skipton, where 9 uPVC windows and 2 exterior wall boxes were professionally resprayed.

The original windows were standard white uPVC—clean but dated, and lacking the modern finish the client wanted. Rather than replacing the windows entirely, the client opted for a cost-effective spray solution to achieve a like-new appearance in a contemporary shade.

Project Details

  • Colour Anthracite Grey (RAL 7016)
  • Finish Satin Sheen (Lechler Paints)
  • Timeline 2 Days On-Site
The Preparation

Why Prep is Key on uPVC

Standard white uPVC can fade and lose its visual appeal over the years. To ensure a brand-new coating bonds perfectly and lasts for years, meticulous preparation is essential.

Rather than replacing perfectly good window frames at a massive expense, a professional respray offers a factory-like finish that is far superior to standard brush or roller applications. We carried out all work onsite over a 2-day period to keep disruption to an absolute minimum.

Attention to Detail: Window openers were carefully removed and taped separately to guarantee sharp, clean edges across every frame.

Our Process

How We Did It

Using a professional HVLP spray system and a specialist coating system from Lechler paints, we followed our strict exterior methodology:

Deep Clean

Each window was deep cleaned and fully degreased to remove all dirt.

Mechanical Key

Surfaces were mechanically keyed to ensure proper paint adhesion.

Masking

Carefully masked to protect surrounding brickwork and glass.

HVLP Spray

Applied Lechler paints for a flawless, durable satin sheen.

The Stunning Result

The client chose Anthracite Grey (RAL 7016)—one of the most popular modern exterior colours. The transformation from white to dark grey gave the property a completely updated look, adding contrast, depth, and a much more contemporary feel to the home's exterior.
